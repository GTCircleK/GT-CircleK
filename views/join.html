Benefits of Circle K International

Service

Circle K focuses on service. Whether cleaning a beach, feeding the homeless, or building a playground, Circle K’ers give their time and efforts to make a difference. Service is perhaps the most rewarding and fulfilling of all Circle K’s tenets because members not only help the world around them but also help themselves grow.

Leadership

​Circle K’ers have various opportunities to serve in leadership positions at the club, district, and international levels. By working on a committee or a board of officers, Circle K’ers develop leadership skills and long-term connections that can benefit them in their professions and post-graduate careers.

Fellowship

Circle K’ers reign from across the world and come together in service and leadership to form bonds that last for years. As a Circle K’er, you will meet people from different walks of life and find friends from different schools and states. With a shared love of service, these friends can last a lifetime.
